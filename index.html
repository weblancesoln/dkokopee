About DKOKOPEE

Singer • Rapper • Songwriter

Koko Pee, also known as D’Lyrical Engineer, is a Nigerian singer, rapper, songwriter, content creator, and activist. His real name is Okokowa Ogaga Jerry, and he was born on December 11, 1994, in Dublin, Ireland. He comes from Oghara-Iyede in Isoko North Local Government Area, Delta State, Nigeria. Koko Pee is popular for his meaningful music that blends Afrobeats, R&B, traditional Nigerian sounds, and messages of hope and social change.
